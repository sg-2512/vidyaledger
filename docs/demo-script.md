# Demo Script

1. Login as Admin.
2. Open Dashboard and show demand, collected, pending, concessions, and defaulters.
3. Open Students and choose Asha Sharma.
4. Show demand, EWS concession, paid amount, and pending amount.
5. Open Fee Engine and generate a term fee for a class.
6. Open Concessions and approve the pending SC concession.
7. Open Payments and record a UPI payment.
8. Download the receipt PDF.
9. Record or select a cheque payment and mark it bounced or cleared.
10. Open Reconciliation and mark an unmatched item as matched or partial.
11. Open Reports and download the collection report.

Suggested closing line:

VidyaLedger gives Indian schools one auditable finance system for fee setup, concessions, collections, reconciliation, and reporting.
