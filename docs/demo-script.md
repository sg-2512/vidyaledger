# Demo Script

1. Login as Admin.
2. Open Dashboard and show demand, collected, pending, concessions, and defaulters.
3. Open Students, show filters, and use Import CSV to preview a small student upload.
4. Choose Asha Sharma and show demand, EWS concession, paid amount, and pending amount.
5. Open Fee Engine and generate a term fee for a class.
6. Open Concessions and approve the pending SC concession.
7. Open Payments and create a UPI Intent or hosted gateway request.
8. Copy the parent-ready reminder message, then copy the raw payment link.
9. Record a UPI payment and download the receipt PDF.
10. Record or select a cheque payment and mark it bounced or cleared.
11. Open Reconciliation, import settlement CSV rows, and apply matched or partial decisions.
12. Open Reports and download the collection report.

Suggested closing line:

VidyaLedger gives Indian schools one auditable finance system for fee setup, concessions, collections, reconciliation, and reporting.
